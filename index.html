Projects

The Last Rolodex

What it does:

The app puts traditional business cards out of business(pun intended) by allowing its users to share and receive contact information by scanning another user's QR code.

What I learned:

How to utilize the qr-scanner library from npm along with React. Sending/receiving data to the API after extracting it from a scanned QR was the highlight of this project.

Technologies Used:

  • React
  • MaterialUI
  • QR-Scanner.js
  • Redux
  • Axios
  • React-Router
  • Node.js
  • Yarn
